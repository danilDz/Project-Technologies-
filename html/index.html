Future Technologies

which inspire the world

A fabulous future: flying cars, human robots, voice-controlled houses, own virtual assistants, food "out of thin air" and printed human organs. We are talking about the most incredible technologies that have already been implemented.
How often as a child have you read books, watched films or cartoons about the future and thought that humanity would never invent a flying skate, folding smartphone or robot that would be completely human?
We don't know about you, but we have had this quite often. Today we decided to collect the most interesting and most incredible technologies, which previously seemed only pictures from films or lines from books, to demonstrate how much humanity is progressing.
